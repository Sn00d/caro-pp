Privacy Policy for Caro (Car.gr Price Analysis Extension)

Effective Date: March 21, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Caro ("we", "our", or "extension") handles data when you use our browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect or transmit any personal information.

Our extension:

  • Does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Does not track browsing history outside of car.gr
  • Does not transmit data to external servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Local Storage

The Caro extension stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • Car listings you view on car.gr
  • Price analyses and comparison data for these listings
  • Your preferences for the extension's functionality
  • History of listings you've viewed for comparison purposes

This data is stored using Chrome's storage API and remains entirely on your device. We use this information solely to provide the extension's core functionality of price analysis and comparison.

Website Access

Our extension only accesses car.gr pages to:

  • Extract information about car listings you browse
  • Analyze car prices by comparing them with other listings
  • Display analysis results directly on the car.gr pages you visit

Data Retention

All data stored by the extension remains on your device until you choose to clear it. You can delete all stored data at any time by using the "Clear Data" function in the extension popup.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension description on the Chrome Web Store.
